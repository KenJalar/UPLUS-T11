About UPLUS International

Who We Are

UPLUS Empowerment and Transformation International (UPLUS International) is a community-based non-governmental organization committed to empowering individuals and transforming communities through innovative, creative, technological, inclusive, and sustainable approaches. Rooted in the belief that every person deserves the opportunity to thrive, UPLUS International works to unlock human potential, reduce inequalities, and foster resilience in the face of today’s pressing challenges.

Our Vision

To build a just and inclusive world where every individual thrives with opportunity, every community achieves sustainable transformation, and global partnerships drive equity, innovation, and lasting impact.

Voices of our Community

Our Mission

We empower children, youth, women, and vulnerable groups by strengthening community empowerment, advancing education, supporting care leavers, promoting mental health, championing climate action, and fostering innovation. By building strong local and global partnerships, we drive transformative change that promotes equity and creates lasting social impact.

Our Impact Cycle Explained

Our work is guided by a data-driven, cyclical process that ensures our interventions are relevant, effective, and sustainable. This strategy allows us to continuously learn and adapt, maximizing our positive impact in the communities we serve.

1
Learning & Capacity Building

Intensive training sessions, workshops, and mentorship to equip community members with foundational knowledge.

2
Skills Application

Participants apply new skills in real-world environments through apprenticeships and group projects.

3
Mentorship

Regular check-ins, coaching, and one-on-one mentorship to strengthen project ideas and address challenges.

4
Evaluation & Recognition

We assess achievements and outstanding participants receive recognition or seed funding to scale their work.

5
Sustainability & Scaling

We ensure long-term success by integrating projects into community structures and fostering local ownership.

Message from the Founder

Ssekitto James

Lead Founder

"As the lead founder of UPLUS Empowerment & Transformation International, my journey is my testimony. I grew up in an institutional care setting in Uganda, where I experienced both the challenges of being an orphan and the resilience that comes from communal care. Transitioning from institutional life into independent living was not easy. I faced uncertainties, financial struggles, and the weight of navigating adulthood without strong safety nets. Yet, from those challenges, I found my purpose to ensure that no young person or vulnerable community member should walk that journey alone. UPLUS is more than an organization. It is a platform of hope. It is a movement where care leavers, young people, women, children, and the elderly can find dignity, support, and opportunities. It is a family where pain is turned into purpose, and every voice is amplified. Through our programs in youth empowerment, vocational skilling, financial inclusion, mental health, and climate action, we are not just responding to problems we are building lasting solutions. My vision is to see a generation of young people who are independent, resilient, and empowered to lead. I want to see women take their rightful place in leadership, children grow up in safety and dignity, and the elderly live with respect and care. This is the heartbeat of UPLUS to transform lives and communities in ways that leave no one behind. I invite you partners, donors, community members, and fellow youth to walk with us. Let us shape a Uganda, and indeed a world, where empowerment is the foundation of every life story. Together, we can build a legacy of transformation that will outlive us. "
