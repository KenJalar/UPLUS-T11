The Power of Community-Led Development

Published on July 15, 2025 by Ssekito James

For too long, development has been a top-down process. At UPLUS, we believe in flipping the script. True, sustainable change doesn't come from external experts dictating solutions; it grows from the grassroots up.

This article explores our recent work in the Kyanja community, detailing how we facilitated the creation of a local development committee. We'll dive into the participatory methods used, the challenges overcome, and the incredible early successes in waste management and youth engagement that have emerged from empowering local leaders to take the reins.

Beyond the Classroom: Holistic Support for Care Leavers

Published on June 02, 2025 by Jalar Kendrick

Transitioning from institutional care to independent adulthood is a journey fraught with unique challenges. While education and vocational skills are vital, they are only part of the puzzle.

This publication shares insights from our 'Support Care Leavers' program, emphasizing the critical role of mental health support, life skills training (like financial literacy and housing), and mentorship. We share personal stories and data that illustrate how a holistic, wrap-around support system is essential for helping these resilient young people not just survive, but truly thrive.
